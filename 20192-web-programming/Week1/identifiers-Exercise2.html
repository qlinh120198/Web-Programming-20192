5 kinds of identifiers

  • Student ID

    • Each student is provided with a student card or a student certificate including a corresponding student ID number.

  • Biometric identifiers

    • Biometric identifiers are the distinctive, measurable characteristics used to label and describe individuals.

  • Electronic identification credentials

    • Electronic authentication is the process of establishing confidence in user identities electronically presented to an information system.

  • Passport Number

    • Vietnamese passports are issued to citizens of Vietnam to facilitate international travel.

  • Individual tax code

    • Any individual present in Vietnam who has taxable income must obtain an individual tax code.
